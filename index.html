The biggest Doomer on Planet Earth.

Doomer is a variation of the Wojak character that is typically characterized as an early 20's male who
suffers from depression and has a bleak outlook on the world, in contrast to the 30 Year-Old Boomer.
The character is often discussed on various 4chan boards in the context of mental health, drug addiction,
economic strife.
